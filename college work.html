ILLUSTRATION - Penguin books
In this task, I was attempting to find my illustration style by drawing on those who influence me, I used the rough hand sketching style of Don Kenn, combined with the digital, darker style of Linas Garsys. In a marriage of the 2 styles I created 3 book covers for popular penguin classics of my choice.

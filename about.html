ABOUT ME

My full name is Nicholas Prodromou.
I am a designer based in Sydney, I've recently completed a diploma in graphic design at CATC.


I've built this website as a platform to launch my freelance work and career. I specialise in branding and communication design, but I have a strong interest in illustration and traditional media.


I'm passionate about design and I believe in the transformative powers of good design, design transcends all language barriers and is capable of connecting to an audience on a deeper level.


As a designer, I strive to make the world a more aesthetically pleasing place, in my time studying at CATC I learned the value of well thought out design, and putting in the time to come to the right solution for the client.
